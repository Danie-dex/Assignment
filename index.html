Who is cooking?

My notebook

Small Batch started as a stack of index cards taped inside a kitchen cabinet. Every recipe here has been made on a weeknight, scaled down for one or two people, and written the way you'd actually explain it to a friend on the phone no stand mixers required, no ingredient you can't find twice.

If something doesn't work in your kitchen, it doesn't belong in the notebook. That's the only editorial standard we've got.
